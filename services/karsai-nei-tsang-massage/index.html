What Is Karsai Nei Tsang?

Karsai Nei Tsang is a bodywork technique inspired by Chi Nei Tsang and the Taoist tradition. It focuses on the lower abdomen, pelvis, and genital area through mindful and respectful manual techniques, with the goal of promoting body awareness, relaxation, and overall well-being. Unlike conventional massage, this technique pays special attention to areas where physical tension and stress are often stored. Each session is provided professionally, with clear communication, informed consent, and complete respect for each person's boundaries, comfort, and personal preferences.

Karsai Nei Tsang massage can also be combined with complementary practices such as tantra massage, creating a more complete and integrated wellness experience. For those interested in a deeper process, it aligns naturally with a broader tantra healing costa rica approach, supporting relaxation, presence, and mind-body connection over time.

Whether you are looking to release tension, improve your sense of balance, or explore a more refined approach to wellness, this therapy offers a unique and effective way to reconnect with your body in a calm, professional, and supportive environment.
