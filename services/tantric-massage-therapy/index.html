Tantric Massage Therapy

Our Tantric Massage Therapy is thoughtfully designed to support deep relaxation, emotional balance, and overall wellbeing through a professional and structured approach. Each session integrates mindful touch, breath awareness, and personalized techniques that help release physical tension, calm the nervous system, and restore a natural sense of inner balance. All services are delivered in a safe, respectful, and discreet environment, ideal for travelers and expats seeking high-quality bodywork in Costa Rica.

We offer mobile, in-hotel sessions in San Jose, conveniently located near Juan Santamaria International Airport (SJO), with extended coverage in Alajuela, Heredia, Escazu, Santa Ana, Cartago, Jaco, and Poas. This flexibility allows you to enjoy a professional massage experience in the comfort and privacy of your accommodation, making it an ideal option after long flights, busy travel schedules, or simply as part of a relaxing wellness routine.

Many clients choose this service as a way to reconnect with their body, reduce stress, and enhance overall wellbeing. Sessions can also be complemented with Karsai Nei Tsang massage, a specialized technique focused on abdominal wellness and energy balance, providing a more complete and integrative bodywork experience.

For those looking to deepen their experience, we also offer tantra retreat in Costa Rica programs designed for extended relaxation, personal growth, and holistic restoration in a calm and supportive environment.
